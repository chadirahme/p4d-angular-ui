Passion for Dessert is a homemade cooking business, operated in Lebanon, Beirut. I started Passion for Dessert in November of 2001 with one goal in mind, to make people who are passionate about dessert happy. I started with traditional homemade recipes of cakes, cookies and muffins that were asked for by family, close friends and neighbors who in turn encouraged me to go for more and to present my recipes to others. So at a later stage I participated in exhibitions and fairs. When my recipes had a great impact on its tasters, I believed that I shall have an outlet for Passion for Dessert ,and I thought of having this family business established and the first step in this one thousand mile journey starts here in my website www.passionfordessert.net

My Mission

To provide the optimum fresh-baked gifts and desserts for all of life's moments, delivered with warmth and backed by my guarantee of excellence, and to let others know the real taste of what a dessert are.

My Vision

To produce the most healthy, original, creative and delicious dessert flavor for any occasion.
